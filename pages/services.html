Specialty Services

Eye Care Services

Al Baseer Hospital's ophthalmology department provides comprehensive eye care with the latest technology and experienced specialists.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification (phaco) surgery with premium foldable intraocular lenses. We perform both traditional and micro-incision cataract surgeries with excellent visual outcomes. Free surgeries available for deserving patients.

Glaucoma Treatment

Comprehensive glaucoma management including medical therapy, laser treatment (SLT, YAG PI), and surgical interventions (trabeculectomy, tube shunt). Early detection through regular screening programs.

Retinal Care

Diagnosis and treatment of retinal disorders including diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration (AMD), retinal detachment repair, and vitreoretinal surgeries.

Vision Correction (LASIK)

Refractive surgery services including LASIK and PRK for correction of myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Comprehensive pre-operative assessment and post-operative care.

Corneal Services

Corneal transplantation (keratoplasty), treatment of corneal infections and dystrophies, and management of keratoconus with cross-linking and ring implantation.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Specialized eye care for children including treatment of strabismus (squint), amblyopia (lazy eye), pediatric cataracts, and routine vision screening for school children.

Oculoplasty

Eyelid surgeries, tear duct procedures (DCR), orbital surgery, and cosmetic procedures around the eyes. Treatment of ptosis, entropion, and ectropion.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Thorough eye examinations including visual acuity testing, refraction, slit lamp examination, fundoscopy, tonometry, and OCT scanning for early detection of eye diseases.

Free Eye Camps

Regular community eye screening camps in rural and underserved areas. Free check-ups, spectacles distribution, and referrals for surgery. Bringing eye care to those who cannot come to us.

General Healthcare

Other Medical Services

Beyond our eye care specialty, Al Baseer Hospital offers a range of general healthcare services.

General Medicine

Outpatient and inpatient medical care for a wide range of conditions including diabetes, hypertension, respiratory diseases, and infectious diseases.

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency department equipped to handle medical emergencies, trauma, eye injuries, and acute conditions requiring immediate attention.

Diagnostic Laboratory

Complete pathology, clinical chemistry, hematology, microbiology, and serology services with accurate and timely results.

Radiology & Imaging

Digital X-ray, ultrasound, OCT scanning, fundus photography, and other imaging modalities for accurate diagnosis.

Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy with affordable medications. Free medicines provided to qualifying patients through our welfare program.

Preventive Healthcare

Health screening programs, vaccination campaigns, diabetes screening, blood pressure monitoring, and community health education initiatives.
