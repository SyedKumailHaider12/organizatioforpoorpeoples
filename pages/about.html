Our Story

From Friends to
Founders of Hope

Organization For Poor People (OFPP) was born from a simple yet powerful idea — that those who have been blessed with wealth and success have a responsibility to uplift those who are less fortunate.

A group of successful, well-established friends from diverse backgrounds came together with a shared vision: to create a world where poverty does not determine the quality of healthcare a person receives. What began as conversations over dinner tables evolved into a formal organization with a clear mission and unwavering commitment.

Today, OFPP stands as a testament to what friendship, compassion, and dedication can achieve. Our founders continue to personally oversee operations, ensuring that every donation, every rupee, and every effort reaches those who need it most.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide free and affordable healthcare services, especially eye care, to underprivileged communities, ensuring that no one is denied treatment due to financial constraints. We believe healthcare is a fundamental human right.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual, regardless of their economic status, has access to quality healthcare. We envision a future free from preventable blindness and treatable diseases that rob the poor of their dignity.

Our Values

Compassion, Transparency, Excellence, and Service Above Self. We operate with complete financial transparency and ensure that the maximum possible resources reach our beneficiaries directly.

The Beginning

Why We Started

The story behind OFPP and what motivated our founders to take action.

Our founders witnessed firsthand how poverty strips people of their basic right to healthcare. They saw children losing their eyesight because their families couldn't afford a simple surgery. They saw elderly people living in darkness, unable to see the faces of their loved ones.


These experiences moved them deeply. Being blessed with financial success and a wide network of influential friends, they decided to pool their resources, knowledge, and connections to create an organization that would directly address these needs. The result was OFPP — and its first and flagship initiative, Al Baseer Hospital.


The name "Al Baseer" (The All-Seeing) was chosen deliberately — representing both the hospital's specialty in eye care and the divine attribute of seeing and caring for all creation. Today, Al Baseer Hospital serves as a beacon of hope, providing world-class eye care and general healthcare to those who need it most.

What Sets Us Apart

Our Core Principles

100% Transparency

Every donation is tracked and accounted for. We publish regular reports showing exactly how funds are utilized in our programs.

Direct Impact

We minimize overhead costs to ensure maximum resources reach the people who need them most. Our founders cover administrative costs.

Excellence in Care

Free doesn't mean substandard. We maintain the highest medical standards with qualified doctors and modern equipment.

Community First

We go to the people through mobile medical camps, community outreach, and satellite clinics in underserved areas.

Dignity & Respect

Every patient is treated with the utmost respect and dignity. We believe in serving humanity without discrimination of any kind.

Sustainable Growth

We plan for long-term impact through training local healthcare workers, building infrastructure, and creating self-sustaining programs.
