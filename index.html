Who We Are

A Mission Born From
Friendship & Compassion

Organization For Poor People (OFPP) was established by a group of successful and well-settled friends who shared a common dream — to make quality healthcare accessible to every human being, regardless of their financial situation.

What started as a heartfelt conversation among friends has grown into a powerful movement that touches thousands of lives. Our founders, blessed with resources and driven by empathy, decided to channel their success into serving those who need it most.

Our Flagship Initiative

Al Baseer Hospital

Specialized in comprehensive healthcare and eye care services, Al Baseer Hospital is OFPP's first and proudest achievement — a beacon of hope for those who cannot afford medical treatment.

About Al Baseer Hospital

Al Baseer Hospital, accessible at albaseerhospital.com, is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility that provides world-class medical services with a special focus on ophthalmology and eye care. The name "Al Baseer" means "The All-Seeing" — reflecting our mission to restore sight and provide vision care to thousands.

Learn More

Eye Care Excellence

Our specialized eye care department offers cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, retinal care, corneal transplants, and comprehensive vision screening programs.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

Al Baseer Hospital provides a comprehensive range of medical services with cutting-edge technology and compassionate care.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced phacoemulsification and traditional cataract removal surgeries with premium intraocular lens implantation.

Glaucoma Treatment

Early detection, medical management, laser therapy, and surgical interventions for all types of glaucoma.

Vision Correction

LASIK eye surgery, corrective lens fitting, and comprehensive vision testing and prescription services.

Retinal Care

Diagnosis and treatment of retinal disorders including diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal detachment.

General Medicine

Comprehensive internal medicine services, health screenings, chronic disease management, and preventive healthcare.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency department equipped to handle medical emergencies, eye injuries, and urgent care needs.
